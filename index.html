Arts Council England Funding

Arts Council England funding per unit population

Arts Council England publishes data about National Lottery Funding Grants. Currently, the data contains grants awarded between April 2018 and August 2022. Each UK grant is associated with a Local Authority. This means an estimate for total funding received per person by Local Authority can be calculated using population statistics from the ONS. For all below figures, population statistics from 2020 have been used.

Overview

  • From the beginning of April 2018 to the end of August 2022 Arts Council England gave a total of 16,397 grants worth a total of £381,009,179.
  • A total of 16,377 went to organisations in the UK, worth a total of £380,736,287.
  • A total of 16,295 went to organisations in England, worth a total of £379,456,030. England has a population of 56,550,138 so this equates to £6.74 per person.
